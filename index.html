Our Policies

planting

We prioritize planting native species to restore local biodiversity and enhance ecosystem resilience. Community-driven planting initiatives foster environmental stewardship among residents. We ensure planting success through regular monitoring and sustainable maintenance.

recycle

We reduce waste through recycling programs for paper, plastics, metals, and electronic waste. Promoting waste segregation maximizes recycling efficiency. Education and outreach empower active participation in recycling initiatives.

water saving

We encourage water-efficient fixtures and practices to conserve this vital resource. Rainwater harvesting supplements freshwater supplies sustainably. Public awareness campaigns educate communities about responsible water use.

tree saving

We advocate for protecting trees and forests to mitigate climate change and preserve habitats. Urban tree protection ensures sustainable management of green spaces. Supporting reforestation projects restores ecosystems and enhances biodiversity.

animals saving

Our policies focus on protecting wildlife habitats and combating threats like habitat loss and illegal trade. Strict measures prevent poaching and promote wildlife conservation. Establishing wildlife corridors maintains species connectivity and survival.

solar panel

Incentivizing solar panel adoption promotes clean energy and reduces carbon emissions. Integrating solar energy into the grid ensures reliable renewable energy access. Researching solar technology advances efficiency and affordability.

Waste Management

We prioritize implementing waste reduction programs, recycling initiatives, and promoting composting to minimize landfill waste and improve resource efficiency.

Ocean Conservation

We developed policies to sustainably manage marine resources and protect ocean ecosystems from pollution, overfishing, and habitat degradation. Establishing marine protected areas, promoting sustainable fishing practices, and investing in research to mitigate ocean acidification and marine biodiversity loss.

Climate Adaptation

Setting emissions reduction targets, supporting carbon capture and storage technologies, and promoting sustainable land use practices to mitigate climate change impacts.

Environmental Education

We are integrating environmental education into school curricula, organizing community workshops, and fostering partnerships to raise awareness and empower individuals to take environmental action.

Energy Efficiency

Developing policies to enhance energy efficiency across all sectors is crucial for reducing greenhouse gas emissions and fostering sustainable energy practices. Our commitment to innovation and collaboration ensures a resilient future focused on minimizing environmental impact and optimizing resource use.

December 2022

THE SEED FOREST ALLIANCE HAS LAUNCHED!

A decentralized global seed bank network can significantly enhance restoration, protect threatened plant species, and expand native seed supply sustainably, benefiting local communities. Currently, 410 seed banks exist across 96 countries, but more are needed. Establishing larger regional seed banks could improve efficiency. The U.S., Australia, Brazil, and China have the most seed banks but also the highest need for new ones to meet future restoration goals. This new alliance will prioritize seed supply and connect forestry groups with necessary funding.

December 2022

1.5 MILLION FRUIT TREES PLANTED FOR SUSTAINABLE AGROFORESTRY

Despite ongoing challenges like local lockdowns and restrictions on movement, our amazing on-ground partner planted a total of 1,533,930 fruit trees to restore 1,553 hectares of land in India last year. The plantings were distributed across the states of Uttar Pradesh, Madya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Haryana, Rajasthan and Uttarakhand, India. We also built nurseries in strategic areas of Haryana to reduce transportation costs and improve seedling acclimatization. As the trees grow, they'll help alleviate hunger and poverty while fighting pollution and climate change across rural India.
